About Dawn and Sunset Cafe

Welcome to Dawn and Sunset Cafe, your go-to coffee shop that started in 2025. Our mission is to provide you with a delicious coffee experience to fuel your everyday grind.

Our Story

Founded by Daisy Abregana and Shane Amigable, Dawn and Sunset Cafe was created to make coffee an essential part of your day. Inspired by the busy lives of working students, the cafe aims to provide a cozy space with affordable coffee and tasty side dishes, like breads and sandwiches, for all those who need a break or a little boost to get through their day.
